Import your bibliography using BibTeX files or DOI references to automate publication listings.

Managing your publications is easier than ever:

- Upload a BibTeX (.bib) file or provide DOIs for your papers.
- Auto-generate publication list pages with formatted citations.
- Connect your ORCID to pull verified publication data automatically.

This automation saves time and ensures consistency across your academic profile.
